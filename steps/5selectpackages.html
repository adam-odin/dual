Browsers

There are many options and this will undoubtedly reflect my preferences for uncluttered experience, privacy and avoiding unnecessary tracking. I think it is an aesthetic preference. If it doesn't serve me, why give it over to others? Feel free to ignore this bit

  • Firefox: comes as standard with Linux. Mozilla open-source
  • Chrome: recommended by The Odin Project
  • Ghostery: my preference
  • Opera: reportedly free VPN within the browser. I tried it then removed it. really liked the side bars on the browser though
  • Edge: Microsoft's default browser. Mercifully the adverts, left-triggering clickbait "news" articles and unnecessary tracking are scarce in a linux environment - it is like having a fresh breeze gently and warmly washing through the house working with linux after getting used to Microsoft edge.
